    Instructions

    Welcome to JSpardy! This game is an online version of the game show, Jeopardy!, written entirely in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. The rules are simple.
    • First, select your categories. You may have as many or as few as you'd like.
    • Next, select if you are playing with one or two players. Once you click 'start game', the game board will appear.
    • Select clues and, for correct answers, you will receive the point value on the clue card. However, incorrect answers will deduct that value from your score.
    • If you feel your answer was misjudged as incorrect, click the "Dispute Answer" button to receive your points back.
